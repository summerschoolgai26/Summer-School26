About the event

Where Deep Learning
meets Medicine

The Summer School on Artificial Intelligence and Generative AI in Medical Applications will explore key areas of AI and deep learning, with a particular focus on healthcare — including medical imaging, clinical data analysis, and generative modeling.

Major challenges and emerging trends will be addressed through lectures, round tables, and hands-on laboratory sessions over four intensive days, providing participants with an interactive and collaborative learning environment.

Organized by the ATSI Association, in collaboration with the IEEE International Conference ATSIP and the Tunisian Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, through Collaborative Research Program PRC2024-D3P03.

Program Overview

A progressive journey from PyTorch fundamentals to state-of-the-art generative models and Vision Transformers, with real medical use cases throughout.

📄 Call for Participation (PDF) 📅 Preliminary Program (PDF)
Day 1 · July 1
PyTorch Foundations, MLP & CNN
  • PyTorch tensors, autograd & nn.Module
  • DataLoader, training loop, state_dict
  • MLP for 1D signal classification (ECG)
  • CNN architectures: ResNet, EfficientNet
  • Transfer Learning & fine-tuning
  • torchvision transforms & augmentation
Day 2 · July 2
Transfer Learning, RNN/LSTM & Explainability
  • Fine-tuning ResNet50 on Chest X-Ray
  • RNN, LSTM, GRU for time series
  • BiLSTM for ECG denoising
  • Explainable AI: Grad-CAM, SHAP, LIME
  • Captum library & Attention Rollout
  • XAI for medical regulatory compliance
Day 3 · July 3
Autoencoders, VAE & Diffusion Models
  • ConvAE for MRI/CT denoising
  • Variational Autoencoder (reparametrization)
  • DDPM: forward/reverse diffusion process
  • U-Net conditioned on timestep
  • DDIM accelerated sampling
  • Hugging Face diffusers library
Day 4 · July 4
GAN, Vision Transformers & Final Project
  • DCGAN, cGAN, Pix2Pix in PyTorch
  • SRGAN for medical super-resolution
  • Vision Transformers: ViT, Swin, DeiT
  • SegFormer semantic segmentation
  • TorchScript & ONNX deployment
  • Final project live demo & evaluation
